Leh
Leh is the capital city of Ladakh, It is located near the eastern border of India, on the ancient trade route known as the Silk Route. It is a popular tourist destination for its cultural and natural attractions.

Chidya-Tapu
Chidiya Tapu, also known as “Sunset Point” and “Bird Island,” is a picturesque location in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands, located about 25 kms from Port Blair. It’s a paradise for bird watchers
Spiti-Valley
Spiti Valley, located in Himachal Pradesh, India, is a cold desert mountain valley known as 'the middle land' between india and Tibet.The valley is famous for its unique topography,monasteries,trekking routes and semi-frozen rivers and lakes.
